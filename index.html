John Doe

Creative Writer, Researcher, Teacher & Multipotentialite

A truly spirited and soulful individual with many different creative pursuits, who channels her creativity and enthusiasm into personal success – that’s Shafrina Khatun for you in a nutshell. She never gets tired of learning, exploring and applying new things. And when it comes to reading and writing something about resourcefulness and self-assertion, she expresses herself with great courage and boldness.

Having tried many different things in life, she finally found herself unswervingly devoted to Yoga, playing the Cello and writing. She believes that the more we mature and grow, the more complicated the challenges we face. “However, the regular repetition of rumination and self-reflection are the only way to redemption,” she says.

Furthermore, she admits to the fact that she is profoundly keen to teach everything she learns, and impart the knowledge she has gained from her colorful life experiences, to the learners concerned. In her words, “I’m overwhelmingly blessed to be a teacher as well.”

SKILL

Initiative towards Humanity

Founder and Managing Director of ‘SaviorUs – Be the Savior yourself,’ a nonprofit organization. SaviorUs started its journey with the dream of creating a network of amiable and like-minded individuals and extending a helping hand to the less fortunate.

Co-founder of Two Steps

Fond of Latin dance forms and music which led to be a co-founder of Latin Dance School in Dhaka, known as Two Steps. Knowing how to dance helps develop ones postures and brings inner joy. Hence, Two steps is an initiative taken by Latin lovers, for Latin lovers.

Teaching for life

Teaching - not just a passion, but also a field of intense affection through the practice of over 10 years. Having mastered the skill of teaching students from different age groups, she has unquestionably earned the title of an ingenious teacher.

Contribution to Literature

Although she has been ascribed as a Confessional Writer by many readers and critiques, she finds pleasure and peace in expressing her thoughts and unbosoming herself through words. Besides, she is a firm believer that it’s highly unhealthy to suppress the spontaneity of overflowing emotions.
